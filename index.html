bakra-eid

Eid al-Adha, also known as Bakra Eid, is an important Islamic festival celebrated by Muslims around the world. It honors the willingness of Prophet Ibrahim (A.S) to sacrifice his son in obedience to Allah’s command. On this day, Muslims offer special prayers, sacrifice animals like goats, cows, or camels, and distribute the meat among family, friends, and poor people. Bakra Eid teaches lessons of sacrifice, kindness, sharing, and helping others.
