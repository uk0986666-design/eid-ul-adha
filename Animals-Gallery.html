Animal Gallery

Goat

Goats are commonly sacrificed on Eid ul Adha. They are easy to care for and loved by many families.

Cow

Cows are large sacrificial animals and their meat is shared among many people and needy families.

Camel

Camels are strong animals and are also sacrificed in many Muslim countries during Eid ul Adha.

Sheep
