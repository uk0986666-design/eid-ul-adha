Eid Spacial Recipe

Enjoy delicious traditional Eid foods with your family.

Biryani

Spicy and delicious rice dish made with meat and spices.

Ingredients

  1. Beef or Mutton
  2. Basmati Chawal
  3. Brown Onions
  4. Kharray Masale & Biryani Masala
  5. yogart

BBQ

Grilled meat full of smoky and spicy flavor.

Ingredients

  1. Beef or Mutton
  2. Kacha Papita
  3. Adrak-Lehsan Paste
  4. BBQ Masala
  5. yogart

Nihari

Traditional slow-cooked meat curry loved on Eid.

Ingredients

  1. Beef Shank
  2. Nihari Masala
  3. Aata
  4. Desi Ghee
  5. Garnish

kababs

Soft and juicy kebabs served with naan and chutney.

Ingredients

  1. Keema
  2. Kacha Papita
  3. Pyaz aur Hari Mirch
  4. Besan
  5. Kabab Masala

Biryani 1 hour
BBQ 50 minutes
Nihari 3 hour
kababs 50 minutes
